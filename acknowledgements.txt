The failing new_value points to the discount factor. In this assignment, the update is not the undiscounted r + max Q(s',a').

Use:

alpha = 1.0

gamma = 0.5

That makes the update:

new_value = old_value + alpha * (next_reward + gamma * next_max - old_value)

With alpha = 1, this becomes:

new_value = next_reward + 0.5 * next_max
